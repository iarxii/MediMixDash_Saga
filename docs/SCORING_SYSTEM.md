# MediMixDash Saga Scoring System

## Overview
The scoring system in MediMixDash Saga rewards efficient pharmacy management, patient satisfaction, and operational excellence. Points are earned through completing prescriptions, maintaining high morale, and minimizing complaints.

## Scoring Components

### 1. Patient Completion Points
- **Completed Prescription**: +50 points per patient
- **Bonus for Speed**: Additional points based on remaining wait time
  - 20+ seconds left: +20 bonus
  - 10-19 seconds left: +10 bonus
  - <10 seconds left: +5 bonus

### 2. Morale and Satisfaction
- **Compliment**: +10 points
- **Complaint**: -20 points
- **Morale Maintenance**: Bonus at shift end based on final morale level
  - 80-100: +100 bonus
  - 60-79: +50 bonus
  - 40-59: +0
  - <40: -50 penalty

### 3. Efficiency Bonuses
- **Batch Completion**: +25 points for completing multiple patients in quick succession
- **Consultant Utilization**: +15 points per active consultant interaction
- **Inventory Management**: +10 points for optimal stock levels

### 4. Penalties
- **Failed Patient**: -30 points
- **Long Wait Times**: -5 points per patient waiting over 45 seconds
- **Stockouts**: -10 points per medication shortage

## Currency System
- Points convert to currency at 1:1 ratio
- Currency can be used for upgrades, new equipment, and staff training
- End-of-shift currency determines performance rating

## Performance Tiers
- **Novice Pharmacist**: 0-499 points/shift
- **Skilled Dispenser**: 500-999 points/shift
- **Expert Manager**: 1000-1499 points/shift
- **Pharmacy Legend**: 1500+ points/shift

## Daily Challenges
- Special events that offer bonus multipliers
- Time-limited tasks for extra rewards
- Achievement unlocks for consistent high performance

## Leaderboards
- Daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards
- Regional and global rankings
- Special badges for milestones
